A Dream Takes Shape

From vision to reality - the journey of creating our alpine sanctuary

The Vision

Nestled in the heart of Saint Gervais Les Bains, our chalet began as a vision to create the perfect alpine retreat. With unobstructed views of the majestic D√¥mes de Miage and Mont Blanc, this location called for something special - a harmonious blend of contemporary design and traditional Savoie architecture.

Every detail was carefully planned to honor the region's heritage while providing modern comfort for discerning guests seeking both adventure and relaxation in the French Alps.

Breaking Ground

Construction began with respect for the alpine environment and local building traditions. Using locally sourced materials and time-honored techniques, we ensured our chalet would feel authentically Savoyard while incorporating thoughtful modern design.

The challenge was maintaining the characteristic features - traditional shutters, wooden balconies, and stone foundations - while creating contemporary interiors that would appeal to today's design-conscious travelers.

Taking Shape

As the timber frame rose against the mountain backdrop, the chalet's character emerged. Traditional Savoie architecture demands respect for proportion and materials - solid wood beams, natural stone, and those distinctive painted shutters that dot the Saint Gervais landscape.

Each phase brought us closer to our vision: a place where guests could experience authentic alpine living with contemporary comfort, whether they came for powder skiing or summer cycling adventures.
